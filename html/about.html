About Us

Capstone Estate agency was established in Manchester by its founder Lucien Gomes in 2010 with the aim to serve local customers. We cover the whole Manchester and surrounding areas. Over the years Capstone has built an understanding of customer needs. Whether you look to rent or buy a home we are experts on the local market and can provide excellent advice and guidance. We are proud to serve everyone who wants to make Manchester their home either short or long term for over 10 years now. Our team of award winning professionals will accompany you at every step of your journey.
